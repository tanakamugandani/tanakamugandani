About Me

My name is Tanaka Mugandani, but you can call me DJ Nak. I'm a passionate DJ and music producer from Zimbabwe, and I'm thrilled to share my love for music with you.

Growing up in Zimbabwe, I was always surrounded by the vibrant rhythms and beats of Afro House and Afro Tech music. These genres spoke to me on a deep level, and I knew from a young age that I wanted to be a part of this world.

As DJ Nak, I've had the privilege of performing at various events and festivals, sharing my unique sound with audiences across Zimbabwe. My music is a fusion of traditional African rhythms with modern electronic beats, creating a truly infectious and energetic vibe.
